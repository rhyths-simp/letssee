Your Responsibility

Let's See is a personal challenge to put your phone down. You are solely responsible for your own use. The challenge is a fun test of self-control — nothing more.

Device & Health

Leaving your phone idle is entirely normal. However, we are not responsible for any overheating, battery drain, or damage that may occur from extended screen-on time via Wake Lock. Use common sense. If your device feels hot, stop. Your phone matters more than your score.

We store only your chosen display name and your best time on a global leaderboard. No account, no email, no tracking. Your name is public on the leaderboard. Choose wisely — don't use your real name if you'd rather stay anonymous.

Fair Play

The app uses motion detection, screen-on enforcement, and tab-switch detection to keep things honest. Attempting to manipulate your score undermines the spirit of the challenge. It's a game about real self-control. Play it that way.
